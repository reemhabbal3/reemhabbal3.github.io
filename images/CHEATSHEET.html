Two ways to add images to your portfolio:
Option A — Drop files into this images/ folder using the exact filenames below, then refresh your browser.
Option B — Click the Edit Mode button (bottom-right of the site), then click any card to upload directly. No filenames needed.

💡 Tips
• Any image format works (.jpg, .png, .webp) — just keep the filename exactly as listed above.
• Images are automatically cropped to fill their card. Landscape images (wider than tall) work best for most slots.
• If you use Edit Mode instead, images are saved in your browser and will persist across sessions — no files needed.
• Want to clear a saved Edit Mode image? Open your browser console and run: localStorage.removeItem('portfolio_SLOTNAME') (replace SLOTNAME with the filename without extension, e.g. avenue-z-ads).
