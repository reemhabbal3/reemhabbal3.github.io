RH · Case Study

GTV
AI-Powered Creative Production

Leveraged generative AI to transform creative production for five ecommerce health & wellness brands — building autonomous agent workflows that scaled output from 30 assets per month to 300+ per week.

300+

Creatives per Week

2×

ROAS on Top Creatives

10×

Production Increase

Challenge

Traditional creative workflows produced only 30 assets per month per client — far too slow for competitive ecommerce advertising that demands rapid testing and iteration. Product rendering accuracy was also critically low, with only ~10% of AI outputs being usable.

Approach

Developed a proprietary prompting formula optimised for the GTV platform using ChatGPT, refined over a month of testing. Built individual autonomous client agents capable of producing 15–20 creatives per session without manual intervention. Solved product rendering by creating a dedicated agent trained on 50+ reference images per client.

Results

Scaled production to 300+ creatives per week across all five clients. Top-performing creatives achieved 2× ROAS or higher, sustained over multiple weeks. Usable output improved from ~10% to 80%. Product feedback to GTV's development team directly influenced platform improvements benefiting all users.
