01 Workflow Case Study Operations

Problem

30 Assets / Month — Not Enough

Traditional creative workflows produced only 30 assets per month per client — far too slow for competitive ecommerce advertising. Product rendering accuracy was critically low at ~10% usable output.

Solution

Autonomous Agent Workflow

Built a proprietary prompting formula for the GTV platform and deployed individual autonomous client agents producing 15–20 creatives per session. A dedicated rendering agent trained on 50+ product reference images solved accuracy at scale.

Result

300+ Creatives / Week

Scaled production to 300+ creatives per week across five clients. Top creatives hit 2× ROAS or higher. Usable output improved from 10% to 80%. Feedback to GTV's dev team directly shaped platform improvements for all users.
